Vacation Spot:Guntur

Guntur is a lively city in southern India, specifically in the state of Andhra Pradesh. It's known for its spicy food, especially a type of chili pepper called the Guntur chili. People in Guntur grow crops like chili peppers, cotton, and tobacco. The city has some cool old places to visit, like the Kondavid Fort and Amaravathi. These places are interesting for tourists and people who like history.
